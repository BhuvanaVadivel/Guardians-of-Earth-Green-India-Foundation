What is Beema Bamboo?

Nature's Wonder Crop

Beema Bamboo is a specially cultivated giant bamboo variant known for its exceptionally fast growth and high commercial value. It's one of the most profitable and environmentally sustainable crops in India.

Rapid Growth

Grows up to 40–50 feet in just 12–18 months

High Carbon Sequestration

Absorbs more CO₂ than regular trees

Fire & Pest Resistant

Natural resistance to fire and pests

150+ Industrial Uses

Highly durable and versatile
